Privacy Policy – Muslim Companion

Effective date: January 21, 2026

Muslim Companion (“the app”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app processes and why.

Information the app processes

  • Location (optional): If you grant location permission, the app uses your approximate or precise location to determine your city, calculate prayer times, and show Qibla direction. You can also enter your city manually.

  • Notifications & exact alarms: Used to deliver prayer reminders, Athan at the correct time, and optional reminders for Azkar and Quran.

  • Media / images (optional): If you use Image Studio, the app may access:

    • Camera (when you choose Take Photo)
    • Photos you select via the system picker (when you choose Upload Image)
    • Images you save to your device (when you tap Save)

  • App settings: Stored on your device (language, calculation method, notification toggles, selected reciter, theme, etc.).

Advertising

The app shows ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect or use data (such as advertising ID, device information, and usage data) to serve and measure ads. This is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy and AdMob’s program policies. You can control ad personalization in your device settings (e.g. Opt out of Ads Personalization in Android).

What the app does NOT do

  • No account creation or login
  • No selling of your personal data to third parties
  • No collection of sensitive personal information beyond what’s needed for the features you enable

Data storage

  • Most data is stored locally on your device (settings and cached content).
  • Downloaded content (Quran pages, audio, backgrounds) may be cached locally to improve performance.

Network access / third‑party services

The app connects to the internet to provide features such as:

  • Prayer times: external API (e.g. Aladhan)
  • Qibla / map tiles: OpenStreetMap tiles (where used)
  • Quran resources, backgrounds, reciter config: remote content (e.g. GitHub‑hosted JSON/images) and reciter audio hosts
  • Advertising: Google AdMob (see Advertising section above)

These providers may receive standard technical information (e.g. IP address) during normal internet use.

In‑app purchases

If you purchase Premium (remove ads), the transaction is processed by Google Play. We do not store or have access to your payment details. Google’s terms and privacy policy apply to those transactions.

Optional crash / incident reports

If you choose to send an incident report (e.g. after a crash), the app may attach diagnostic logs to an email. This is optional and under your control. We use such information only to help fix issues.

Permissions (why we ask)

Permission Purpose
Location Prayer times and Qibla based on your location
Notifications Prayer reminders, Athan, Azkar & Quran reminders
Exact alarms Trigger Athan at exact prayer time

Children’s privacy

The app is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at the same policy URL. The effective date at the top will be revised when we make changes.
