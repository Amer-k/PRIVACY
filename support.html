Frequently asked questions

Prayer times are wrong or missing. Make sure location access is allowed, or set your city and country in Settings. You can also change the calculation method in Settings if needed.

How do I get Premium / remove ads? Open the app, go to Settings, and tap the Premium option to subscribe or purchase a lifetime upgrade.

I have a billing or subscription question. For refunds or subscription management, use your device’s App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) settings. For other billing help, email us at the address above.

Can I use the app in Arabic? Yes. You can switch the app language in Settings.
