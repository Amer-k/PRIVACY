Privacy Policy – Muslim Companion

Effective date: January 21, 2026

Muslim Companion (“the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

Information the app processes

  • Location (optional): If you allow location access, the app uses your approximate or precise location to determine your city, calculate prayer times, and show Qibla direction. You may also choose to enter your city manually instead.
  • Notifications (optional): If you allow notifications, the app uses them to deliver prayer reminders and Athan alerts at the appropriate times.
  • Media / images (optional): If you use Image Studio, the app may access:
    • Camera, when you choose Take Photo
    • Photos you select through the system photo picker, when you choose Upload Image
    • Images you choose to save to your device, when you tap Save
  • App settings: Preferences such as language, calculation method, notification options, selected reciter, theme, and similar settings are stored locally on your device.

Advertising

The app may display advertisements, including banner and full-screen ads, provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect or use data such as device information, advertising identifiers, and usage data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers. You may remove ads by upgrading to Premium through an in-app purchase, if offered.

What the app does not do

  • No required account creation or login to use the main features of the app
  • No sale of your personal data to third parties
  • No collection of sensitive personal information beyond what is needed for the features you choose to use

Data storage

  • Most app data, such as settings and cached content, is stored locally on your device.
  • Downloaded content, such as Quran pages, audio files, and backgrounds, may be cached locally to improve performance.
  • We do not store your payment card or billing details.

Network access / third-party services

The app connects to the internet to provide features such as:

  • Prayer times: external API services such as Aladhan
  • Qibla map tiles: OpenStreetMap tiles, where used
  • Quran resources, backgrounds, and reciter configuration: remote content such as hosted JSON, images, and audio
  • Advertising: Google AdMob

During normal internet communication, these services may receive standard technical information such as your IP address, device type, and general request metadata.

In-App Purchases

Muslim Companion may offer optional Premium features through in-app purchases, such as removing advertisements. Purchases are processed securely by Apple through your Apple ID using the App Store. We do not store, process, or have access to your payment details.

Permissions (why we ask)

  • Location: to calculate prayer times and show Qibla direction based on your location
  • Notifications: to send prayer reminders and Athan alerts
  • Camera: to take photos for Image Studio
  • Photos: to select or save images for Image Studio
  • Internet access: to retrieve prayer times, content, updates, and ads

Children’s privacy

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address the issue.

Terms of Use

If the app offers subscriptions, they automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. Your Apple ID account may be charged for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current period.

You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings after purchase.
